Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

How quickly can I get an appointment?

We typically offer appointments within 48-72 hours for routine care, and same-day emergency appointments when needed.

Do you accept NHS patients?

Yes, we provide both NHS and private services. NHS eligibility depends on your specific circumstances and location.

What areas do you cover for home visits?

We cover West Yorkshire, North Yorkshire, Greater Manchester, Lancashire, Cumbria, Tyne & Wear, County Durham, Lincolnshire, East Riding, and selected areas of Sheffield. Use our postcode checker on the homepage to confirm if we serve your specific area.

How much do your services cost?

Our prices vary by service type and whether you're NHS or private. Contact us for a detailed quote based on your needs.

What should I expect during a home visit?

Our optometrist will bring all necessary equipment for a comprehensive eye examination in the comfort of your home.

Can you help with contact lens fittings?

Yes, we provide complete contact lens services including initial fittings, follow-ups, and ongoing care.
