Cookie Policy

Information about how we use cookies on our website

Last Updated: September 2025

Effective Date: September 2025

Next Review: September 2026

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience by remembering your preferences and understanding how you use our site.

2. How We Use Cookies

I Care Service Providers Ltd uses cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Understand how you navigate our website
  • Improve website performance and functionality
  • Provide personalized content and services
  • Analyze website traffic and usage patterns
  • Ensure website security

3. Types of Cookies We Use

3.1 Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly and cannot be disabled.

  • Session Cookies: Remember your actions during a single browsing session
  • Security Cookies: Help protect against security threats
  • Form Cookies: Remember information you've entered in forms

3.2 Performance Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website.

  • Analytics Cookies: Collect information about website usage
  • Speed Cookies: Help us optimize website loading times
  • Error Tracking: Help us identify and fix technical issues

3.3 Functionality Cookies

These cookies enhance your experience by remembering your preferences.

  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and choices
  • Language Cookies: Remember your language preferences
  • Accessibility Cookies: Remember accessibility settings

3.4 Marketing Cookies

These cookies help us provide relevant content and measure advertising effectiveness.

  • Targeting Cookies: Help deliver relevant content
  • Social Media Cookies: Enable social media integration
  • Advertising Cookies: Measure and improve advertising effectiveness

5. Your Cookie Choices

5.1 Cookie Consent

When you first visit our website, you will see a cookie banner allowing you to:

  • Accept all cookies
  • Reject non-essential cookies
  • Customize your cookie preferences
  • Learn more about our cookie use

5.2 Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through:

  • Browser Settings: Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies
  • Cookie Preference Center: Use our preference center to manage cookie types
  • Third-Party Tools: Use browser extensions or privacy tools

5.3 Browser Cookie Settings

Here's how to manage cookies in popular browsers:

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Cookies and website data
  • Edge: Settings > Cookies and site permissions > Cookies and site data

6. Impact of Disabling Cookies

If you disable cookies, you may experience:

  • Reduced website functionality
  • Need to re-enter information repeatedly
  • Less personalized experience
  • Inability to use certain features
  • Slower website performance

7. Third-Party Services

7.1 Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand website usage. Google Analytics uses cookies to:

  • Track visitor behavior and demographics
  • Measure website performance
  • Generate usage reports

You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

7.2 Social Media Integration

Our website may include social media features that use cookies:

  • Social sharing buttons
  • Embedded content (videos, posts)
  • Login integration

These features are governed by the privacy policies of the respective social media platforms.

8. Cookie Retention

Different cookies are stored for different periods:

  • Session Cookies: Deleted when you close your browser
  • Persistent Cookies: Remain until they expire or you delete them
  • Essential Cookies: Stored for the minimum time necessary
  • Analytics Cookies: Typically stored for 1-2 years

9. Mobile Apps

If we develop mobile applications, they may use similar tracking technologies:

  • Device identifiers
  • App usage analytics
  • Push notification tokens
  • Location data (with permission)

10. Data Protection

Cookie data is processed in accordance with:

  • Our Privacy Policy
  • UK GDPR requirements
  • Data Protection Act 2018
  • ePrivacy Regulations

11. International Transfers

Some third-party services may transfer cookie data internationally:

  • We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place
  • Transfers comply with UK data protection laws
  • We regularly review third-party practices

12. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect cookies from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe a child has provided cookie data, please contact us immediately.

13. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy to reflect:

  • Changes in technology
  • Legal requirement updates
  • New cookie implementations
  • Changes in third-party services

We will update the "Last Updated" date and notify users of significant changes.

14. Your Rights

Under data protection law, you have rights regarding cookies:

  • Right to be informed: About our cookie use (this policy)
  • Right to object: To non-essential cookie processing
  • Right to access: Information about cookies we've set
  • Right to erasure: Request deletion of cookie data
  • Right to withdraw consent: Change your cookie preferences anytime
