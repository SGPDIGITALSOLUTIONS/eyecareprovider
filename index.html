What Does Professional Home Eye Care Include?

We bring comprehensive eye health services directly to your home, providing the same clinical standards as a traditional practice with the added convenience of not having to travel.

Comprehensive Eye Examinations

Complete eye health assessments using professional diagnostic equipment to detect and monitor eye conditions.

Prescription and Dispensing

Professional eyewear consultations with fitting services and access to premium lens technologies including Hoya lenses.

Specialized Care Services

MECS services, glaucoma monitoring, and diabetic eye screening for ongoing eye health management.

Advanced Care Plan

Subscription-based comprehensive eye care with regular check-ups and priority booking for ongoing eye health.

Equipment and Technology

State-of-the-art portable equipment including digital retinal cameras and visual field testing devices.

Professional Consultation

Expert advice on eye health, prescription updates, and personalized care plans tailored to your specific needs.
