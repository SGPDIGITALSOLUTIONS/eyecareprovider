Comprehensive Eye Care Services

Professional eye health services delivered with expertise, convenience, and care

Expert Eye Care at Your Convenience

We provide a complete range of professional eye care services, bringing expert clinical care directly to your home or offering appointments at our clinic.

👁️

Comprehensive Eye Examinations

Thorough eye health assessments including vision testing, retinal examination, intraocular pressure measurement, and full ocular health screening.

  • Digital retinal photography
  • OCT scanning (when required)
  • Visual field testing
  • Prescription updates
NHS: Free with valid HC2/HC3
Private: £45-65
🏠

Home Visit Eye Tests

Professional eye examinations conducted in the comfort of your own home using portable clinical equipment and maintaining full clinical standards.

  • Full portable equipment setup
  • Same clinical standards as clinic
  • Convenient for mobility issues
  • Safe and hygienic procedures
NHS: Free if eligible
Private: £65-85
👓

Spectacle Dispensing

Expert advice on frame selection, lens options, and precise fitting. Wide range of quality frames and advanced lens technologies.

  • Designer and budget frames
  • Varifocal and single vision lenses
  • Anti-glare and blue light protection
  • Professional fitting and adjustments
🔍

Contact Lens Services

Contact lens consultations, fittings, and ongoing aftercare. All major brands available with expert guidance on lens selection.

  • Initial contact lens consultation
  • Soft and rigid gas permeable lenses
  • Specialty lenses (toric, multifocal)
  • Ongoing aftercare and check-ups
🩺

Diabetic Eye Screening

Specialized diabetic retinopathy screening and monitoring for patients with diabetes, helping prevent vision-threatening complications.

  • Digital retinal photography
  • Diabetic retinopathy assessment
  • Annual monitoring programs
  • Referral coordination when needed
👶

Children's Eye Care

Specialized pediatric eye examinations designed to be comfortable and engaging for children of all ages.

  • Child-friendly examination techniques
  • Visual development assessment
  • Lazy eye and squint detection
  • Educational and fun approach
🚨

Emergency Eye Care

Urgent eye care for sudden vision changes, eye injuries, flashing lights, or other concerning eye symptoms.

  • Same-day appointments when possible
  • Foreign body removal
  • Urgent referrals when required
  • Emergency advice and triage
⚕️

Low Vision Assessment

Specialized assessments and support for patients with reduced vision, providing aids and strategies to maximize remaining sight.

  • Magnification assessments
  • Low vision aid recommendations
  • Adaptation strategies and training
  • Referrals to support services
🎯

Specialized Consultations

Expert consultations for complex eye conditions, second opinions, and specialized assessments requiring advanced clinical expertise.

  • Glaucoma monitoring
  • Macular degeneration assessment
  • Dry eye evaluation
  • Post-surgical follow-up
